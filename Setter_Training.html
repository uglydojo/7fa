📞 THE 12-STEP SETTER CALL

Tight. 8-10 minutes. Never longer.

OPEN
QUALIFY
CREATE DESIRE
BOOK
LOCK IN
1

Greeting & Permission

Confirm identity, introduce yourself, ask for two minutes.

"Hey, is this [Name]? This is [You] with Seven Figure Agency. Do you have a quick two minutes? I promise to keep this short."
2

Reason for Call

Resource check. Confirm they got the free resource they opted in for.

"The reason I'm calling is to make sure you got access to the Roadmap Implementation Program. Did you get that okay?"
3

Clarify Motivation

Why did they opt in? What's their current situation?

"Out of curiosity, what made you download and sign up in the first place?"
4

Label Their Situation

Mirror back what they said. Single obstacle triage: leads, conversion, or systems?

"Sounds like you're feeling [emotion] because you've been spending too much time IN the business instead of ON it. Does that sound right?"
5

Light Qualification

Get the THREE data points. This is where the gates happen.

"Just so I can get a clear snapshot: What niche do you serve? How many clients/locations are you managing? What's your approximate MRR?"
6

Sell the Vision

One question. Let them paint the picture of their future.

"If you had solid systems in place to solve [their issue], what would your business look like 3-6 months from now?"
7

Temperature Check

1-10 scale. Below 8 = push for what would make it a 9 or 10. Above 8 = they're ready.

"On a scale of 1 to 10, where 10 means you're ready to fix this yesterday, where would you say you are right now?"
8

Offer the Session

Transition to the Acceleration Session. One of our experts will walk them through it.

"Based on what you've shared, you'd be a great fit for our complimentary Agency Acceleration Session."
9

Commitment Question

Investment readiness. Are they prepared to invest if the plan makes sense?

"If you knew we could help you fix this and give you a clear roadmap, are you prepared to invest in your business to make it happen?"
10

Pick Day/Time

Give two options. Confirm timezone. Repeat the confirmed slot.

"He's available on [Option 1] or [Option 2]. Which works better for you?"
11

Natural Disaster Question

Lock commitment to the time. The answer should be nothing stops them.

"Is there anything outside of a natural disaster that would stop you from being there for that session?"
12

Device-Ready + Prep Guide

Accept the invite together. Send the prep guide. Confirm they'll review it before the call.

"I'm sending the invite now with the Zoom link. Are you in front of your email so we can accept it together?"

GHOST SETTER vs GATEKEEPER

👻 Ghost Setter

  • 📋 Books anyone with a pulse on the calendar
  • 🔵 Leaves blue (auto-booked) appointments untouched
  • 🤷 Doesn't know client count, MRR, or niche
  • 📱 Sends one text, gives up
  • 😴 No confirmation process, hopes they show up
  • 🎰 Routes high-revenue leads to Esteban without checking client count

🛡️ Gatekeeper

  • 🔍 Qualifies every lead through all three gates before booking
  • ✅ Clears blue appointments by end of day, every day
  • 📊 Has exact client count, MRR, and niche for every set
  • 📲 7+ touchpoints across call, text, and email
  • 🔒 Full confirmation sequence: day before + morning of
  • 🎯 Routes by client count first, revenue second

🎨 CALENDAR COLOR SYSTEM

Every appointment must have a color. No exceptions.

BLUE - Auto-Booked

No human contact yet. MUST be worked.

DARK BLUE - Qualified

Spoke with them. Meets all criteria.

DARK GREEN - Confirmed (Day Before)

Reconfirmed the day before. Ready.

DARK GRAY - Confirmed (Same Day)

Confirmed same day of appointment.

YELLOW - Hot Lead

Had a Strategy Session. Follow up to close.

RED - Not a Fit / DQ

Doesn't qualify. Remove or reassign.

LIGHT GREEN - Closed

Deal closed. Won.

PURPLE - No Show

Didn't attend. Must be followed up.

⚠️ THE #1 RULE

Blues cannot be on the calendar the day of the call.

If you see a blue appointment for today or tomorrow, that is YOUR responsibility.
Work it, qualify it, confirm it, or remove it. No blue goes to the closer.

A blue on the calendar = a ghost appointment = wasted closer time.

⏰ THE DAILY RHYTHM

Hunt Mode: your entire day is about one thing. Filling the calendar with qualified appointments.

FIRST 15 MINUTES

Morning Confirmation Blitz

  • Send same-day confirmation to ALL today's appointments
  • Include Zoom link in every confirmation
  • Update colors: replied = Dark Gray, already confirmed = stays Dark Green
  • Flag any blues that slipped through, work them NOW
NEXT 2 HOURS

New Lead Follow-Up

  • Call every lead from the past 24-48 hours
  • Double dial (call twice back-to-back)
  • If no pickup, text immediately (see text scripts below)
  • Qualify on the call using the Three Gates
MID-DAY

Future Appointment Confirmation

  • Review tomorrow's calendar for blues
  • Send confirmation texts for appointments 2-3 days out
  • 5x call burst for unresponsive leads (5 calls in 1-2 minutes)
  • Unresponsive after multiple attempts = consider removing to free the slot
THROUGHOUT THE DAY

Outbound + Work Your List

  • Work through aged leads in CRM
  • Run qualification calls on leads in pipeline
  • Document everything in GoHighLevel
  • Use the outbound text script for no-pickups
END OF DAY (Last 30 min)

Night Before Process

  • Review all of tomorrow's appointments
  • Send day-before confirmations to every appointment
  • Pre-qualify any unconfirmed blues for tomorrow
  • Update all calendar colors based on responses
  • Log all activity in CRM

✋ BEFORE YOU BOOK ANY SET, YOU NEED:

1. Exact client/location count (not "a few" or "several")
2. Exact monthly revenue (not "significantly over five figures")

If you don't have all three, it's not a set. Don't book it. Get the info first.
